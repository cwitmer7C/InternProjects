Intern Video Project 2026
Video Ideas
2–3 min  ·  filmed in office  ·  low budget
Concept 01
The Concept
Employees are "castaways" competing to survive the work week. Tribal councils in the conference room. Immunity challenges. The prize: the good parking spot.
Potential Moments
Dramatic arrival narrated like stepping onto an island
Immunity challenge — longest to survive a meeting without checking their phone
Tribal council vote in the conference room
Host dramatically unplugs the losing employee's laptop
Slow walk of shame out of the office
Production Needs
LocationsOffice, conf. room
Cast4–6 employees
PropsNone
CostumesOptional — bandanas
Concept 02
The Concept
Contestants compete to create gourmet dishes using only break room ingredients — instant oatmeal, leftover birthday cake, the mystery Tupperware no one claims. Judges critique with full MasterChef seriousness.
Potential Moments
Mystery box dramatically unveiled from a brown paper bag
Contestants frantically "cooking" with a microwave and kettle
Slow motion close-ups of granola bars plated on paper towels
Judges tasting with long dramatic pauses and stern silence
Winner receives a laminated golden spatula
Production Needs
LocationsBreak room, desk area
Cast3–5 employees + 2 judges
PropsBrown bags, paper towels
CostumesChef hats (~$5 on Amazon)
Concept 03
The Concept
A serious David Attenborough-style voiceover narrates completely ordinary office behavior as if it were a nature documentary. Employees act completely natural — the comedy is entirely in the narration.
Potential Moments
Someone making coffee — narrated like approaching a watering hole
Eating lunch alone at a desk — a solitary survival mechanism
A long meeting — a mysterious ritual with no clear purpose
Printer jam — a colony-wide crisis no one has ever solved
5pm — a great migration into the wild
Production Needs
LocationsEntire office
CastWhole office, candid
PropsNone
CostumesNone
